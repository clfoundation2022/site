Margarita Guerra

Margarita, a 13-year-old girl with a congenital malformation will travel from Peru to New York for surgery.

Now a very important process is beginning for you to have quality of life in Peru. We, as a foreign consular office in New York, have a commendable role, which is to ease the pain of our vulnerable community. In the case of Margarita, receiving her in New York is an honor and we are going to contribute to her improvement process and the fact that I am present here generates a very special feeling in her mother, Zenaida, and in her daughter.
